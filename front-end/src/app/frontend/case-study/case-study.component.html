One of the most important tasks as a real estate agent is to price a home correctly for buyers, sellers, and investors. The challenge is to take the emotion out of the pricing decision and to provide evidence with accurate and current data to support the home valuation.

The existing tools provided to real estate agents today to help them price homes fall into two methods. The first is made up of Automated Valuation Models (AVMs) that even consumers can find on-line. These AVMs can be wildly inaccurate. There is little to no input from experienced real estate agents and it solely relies on internal algorithms to select comps and determine value. The second method is one in which the agent comes up with their own systems and math to use along with generally fewer hand picked comps. This method can be very time consuming. It also lacks the ultimate accuracy desired and can be difficult to explain value to the client.

The cmaIQ Pricing Tool was developed by real estate agents to combine the advantages of both methods. The agent is involved in selecting the comps but able to use a broader spectrum that provides the data required to make an accurate assessment. The algorithms in the background help the agent with adjusting the property value for seasonality and market conditions as well as features of the subject property. The program provides a visual display of the comps to help convey pricing to clients.

The analytics help to explain the market trend and then calculate a suggested list price, projected number of days on the market, and probability of sale. The interactive analysis of a client’s property is completed in only minutes without an agent ever having to use a calculator.

The development of this innovative pricing tool quickly caught the eye of agents in a Charleston, SC real estate firm. The office fully integrated the pricing tool in January 2018 to enable access for all of their agents. The results of the first year of use reflect the ability of the tool to help agents price more accurately and convey value to their clients. The numbers are superior across the board.

The proof of the distinct advantages the tool has provided to the agents in the office is in the numbers. The knowledge of agents combined with the cmaIQ data and analytics drive the tool to accurately price homes in a matter of minutes. The cmaIQ presentation allows agents to convey to their clients market value and explain market conditions, seasonality, and time expected to sell.
